About Me

Hi, it's great that you've found your way here to my portfolio page! My name is Joonatan and I'm a fourth year computer science student at Aalto University. I'm a long time programmer and I have more than 5 years of experience in the software development industry. Currently I specialize in full-stack web development, but in the future I would like to develop other kinds of software as well. I have been programming in my spare time since junior high school, and during this time I have gained a lot of experience in different software projects.

In my free time I like to go outdoors, play the piano and play badminton. I also participate in lot of guild activities, and this year I'm the fuksi captain of the computer science guild at Aalto! As the fuksi captain, my job is to introduce new students to the Finnish teekkari culture and student life at Aalto University. In the past I have also been the chair of the guild's digital services committee and been active in guild activities.
